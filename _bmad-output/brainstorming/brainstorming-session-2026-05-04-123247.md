## Technique Execution Results

### Phase 1: SCAMPER — S (Substitute)

**[S #1]: Auto-Stack**
_Concept:_ Replace one-time checkout with a subscription model — users set their supplement stack and the platform auto-replenishes on each product's natural consumption cycle (protein ~30 days, creatine ~40 days).
_Novelty:_ First recurring revenue model in UZ sports nutrition. → **Deprioritized for MVP; purchase model chosen as foundation.**

**[S #2]: Goal-First Discovery**
_Concept:_ Substitute the standard product catalog with a goal-driven discovery engine. User enters their goal — "gain muscle," "lose fat," "improve endurance" — and the platform surfaces a curated, ranked product set with plain-language explanations of why each product fits.
_Novelty:_ Every competitor shows a product grid. This platform speaks to the goal. Transforms a store into a guide.

**[S #3]: Confusion Resolver**
_Concept:_ A dedicated feature handling the top 10–15 conflicting goal questions — "build muscle vs. lose fat," "creatine vs. pre-workout — do I need both?" — with structured plain-language answers that end in a product recommendation and purchase path.
_Novelty:_ Competitors sell products. This feature sells clarity. Builds trust faster than any discount in a low-literacy supplement market.

**[S #4]: Telegram-as-Sidekick**
_Concept:_ Beautiful web platform as the core experience; Telegram bot handles only async touchpoints — order updates, restock alerts, flash sale pings, support tickets.
_Novelty:_ Competitors live inside Telegram. This platform uses Telegram to enhance a superior web experience.

**[S #5]: Demo Videos**
_Concept:_ Short demo videos on product pages — how to mix, when to take, what to expect.
_Novelty:_ Visual trust-building for a market where counterfeit fears are high.

**[S #6]: Waitlist + Restock Notify**
_Concept:_ Substitute "out of stock" dead ends with a waitlist and automatic restock notification.
_Novelty:_ Captures demand that currently leaks to competitors during inventory gaps.

**[S #7]: Gym Pickup Points**
_Concept:_ Partner with Tashkent gyms as pickup locations alongside home delivery.
_Novelty:_ Meets users at their natural habitat; reduces last-mile delivery cost.

**[S #8]: Verified Purchase Badges + Photo Reviews**
_Concept:_ Verified purchase badges and real buyer photo reviews on every product.
_Novelty:_ Directly addresses counterfeit fears — social proof anchored to real transactions.

**[S #9]: English-First UI**
_Concept:_ Platform launches English-only for MVP; Uzbek and Russian added post-launch.
_Novelty:_ Signals quality and international-grade standards matching how target users already consume fitness content.

### Phase 2: COMBINE + Global Best Practices

**Discovery & Navigation**
- Smart search — understands goals, not just keywords
- Filter by goal, brand, price, form (powder/capsule/bar)
- Bestsellers + Trending sections
- "Complete your stack" upsell at checkout

**Product Pages**
- Science tab — plain-English "why this works"
- Nutrition facts / label display
- Authenticity certificates displayed openly
- Product comparison tool
- Low stock urgency indicators
- Influencer reviews embedded on product pages
- Community Q&A on product pages

**Trust & Social Proof**

- Before/after results from real customers

**Purchase & Payments**

### Session Highlights

**Key Insight:** The platform's core value proposition is being a *guide*, not just a store — Goal-First Discovery and the Confusion Resolver are the features that separate it from every competitor in the market.

**Strategic Decision:** Purchase model over subscription for MVP — build trust first, subscriptions are a natural Phase 2 upgrade.

**Platform Positioning:** Beautiful website as primary experience; Telegram and email as supporting service channels. English-first signals premium quality aligned with how target users already consume fitness content.
